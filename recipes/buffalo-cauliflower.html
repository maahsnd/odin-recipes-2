Buffalo Cauliflower

Return Home

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 3/4 cup milk or milk alt
  • 1 head Cauliflower
  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  3. In a large bowl, combine flour, paprika, garlic poweder, salt, pepper and milk
  4. Break Cauliflowerhead into florets 1 & 1/2 in wide
  5. Add florets to batter, ensuring each piece is evenly coated
  6. Arrange coated Cauliflower on the baking sheet
  7. Bake for 20 minutes, flipping halfway
  8. In a small bowl, combine buffalo sauce, coconut oil, honey. Stir until evenly combined
  9. Brush buffalo sauce mix onto Cauliflower and bake for another 20 mintes
